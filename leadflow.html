Leadflow Review: An In-Depth Overview

leads marketing real estate Real estate tool

Leadflow is a leading company in AI-powered real estate technology that is revolutionizing the way real estate professionals conduct business. With the help of artificial intelligence and a state-of-the-art genetic algorithm, Leadflow assigns three distinct Sellability Scores to each property, which predict the likelihood of the property being sold. This innovative approach empowers real estate professionals to make informed and efficient investment decisions.

The Sellability Scores cater to different aspects of real estate, making Leadflow a versatile tool. The three scores, which range from 0 to 1,000, are as follows:

- Retail Score: This score is perfect for residential real estate agents, brokers, and investors. It indicates the likelihood of the property selling within the next 90 days.

- Rental Score: This score is ideal for landlords and investors who want to expand their rental portfolios. It predicts the likelihood of the property being sold and used as a rental property.

- Wholesale Score: This score is designed for wholesalers, fix & flippers, and investors seeking discounted properties. It indicates the likelihood of the property selling at a lower price.

Leadflow's technology is designed to help real estate professionals work smarter, not harder. By focusing on AI Scored leads, businesses have witnessed a significant increase in response rates - an impressive 271% in Leadflow's tests. Additionally, Leadflow's technology saves time and money by filtering leads based on the Sellability Score, allowing marketing efforts to be directed only towards those leads that are most likely to sell.

Leadflow offers comprehensive marketing tools, including direct mail, skip tracing, and SMS campaigns, in addition to data. Its features are designed to ensure that the right message is delivered to the right people at the perfect time, increasing the chances of turning targeted leads into profitable deals.

Leadflow is a versatile solution suitable for a variety of real estate professionals such as flippers, real estate agents, wholesalers, landlords, Airbnb/STR owners, real estate brokers, mortgage brokers, and appraisers. With its vast data resources, comprehensive filtering system, and targeted marketing tools, Leadflow is helping real estate professionals across the spectrum leverage technology to supercharge their businesses.
